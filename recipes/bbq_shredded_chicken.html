BBQ Shredded Chicken Recipe

A tasty meal ready to eat by dinner time!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb boneless, skinless chick breast
  • 2 cups bbq sauce (your choice)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. In a medium prep bowl, add the bbq sauce, brown sugar and apple cidar vinegar and stir
  2. Add the smoked paprika, garlic powder and red pepper flakes and stir
  3. Put sauce aside and preheat slow cooker on low heat for 30 minutes
  4. Spray inside of slow cooker with cooking oil spray (Pam will do) and add the chicken to the slow cooker
  5. Pour the sauce in to the slow cooker, using a spoon to cover the chicken with the sauce
  6. Cook on low for 6 to 7 hours
  7. After cooking, use two forks to shred the chicken
  8. Let shredded chicken sit in cooker for an additional 30 minutes
