Turkey Chili Recipe

A delicious and healthy option of chili!

Hassle free meal ready for dinner!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey meat
  • 1 15.5 oz. can petite diced tomatoes
  • 1 8 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons black pepper

Steps

  1. Low to medium-low heat, toss in the cooking oil and yellow onion in a large pot and simmer for about 2 minutes
  2. Place the ground turkey meat in pot and cook until browned, about 8-10 minutes
  3. Add in the chili powder, garlic powder, ground cumin, salt and pepper and stir until covered
  4. Slowly pour in the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and stir
  5. Turn up heat to medium, medium-high and stir until a slight boil and then turn down heat to low, medium-low heat
  6. Continue to cook and stir occasionally for 30 minutes
